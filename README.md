# ACM VIT Chennai

![ACM VIT Chennai](assets/meta_img.webp)

## About

ACM VIT Chennai is the student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at VIT University, Chennai. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence in computing and technology.

## Mission

To promote computing as a discipline and engage students in activities that advance knowledge and professional development in computer science and related fields.

## What We Do

- Organize technical workshops and seminars
- Host coding competitions and hackathons
- Facilitate networking with industry professionals
- Provide mentorship and career guidance
- Build a community of passionate technologists
